E-Services User Guide

Welcome to the Multi Tax Solution E-Services platform. To get started, you need to:

  1. Register for an account if you don't already have one
  2. Log in with your username and password
  3. Set up two-factor authentication for enhanced security
  4. Update your profile with your personal and contact information

To file a tax return online:

  1. Navigate to the Filing section
  2. Select the tax type you want to file
  3. Choose the applicable tax period
  4. Fill in the required information
  5. Upload any supporting documents
  6. Review and submit your return

To make a tax payment:

  1. Navigate to the Payment section
  2. Select the tax return or liability you want to pay
  3. Choose your preferred payment method
  4. Enter the payment amount
  5. Complete the payment process
  6. Save or print your payment receipt
